= Creating and configuring the conversion hosts

The migration is performed by software installed on the conversion hosts.

Creating and configuring conversion hosts involves the following key steps:

. Deciding xref:Number_of_conversion_hosts[how many conversion hosts] you need
. Choosing xref:datapath_transformation_options_vddk_ssh[VDDK or SSH] as the transformation option
. Creating the xref:Creating_RHV_conversion_hosts[Red Hat Virtualization] or xref:Creating_OSP_conversion_hosts[OpenStack Platform] conversion hosts
. Configuring the conversion hosts for xref:Configuring_conversion_hosts_for_transformation[VDDK or SSH transformation]
. Enabling the conversion hosts in xref:Enabling_conversion_hosts_in_cloudforms[CloudForms]
. Optionally, changing the xref:Configuring_the_maximum_number_of_concurrent_migrations[maximum number of concurrent migrations]
. Validating the xref:Verifying_conversion_hosts[conversion hosts]
